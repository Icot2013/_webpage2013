Activities                                                                                                         News
The International Club of Trondheim (ICOT) is a non-profit, non-political and purely a social organization of the students and their families, established by a group of international students studying at NTNU.
ICOT IS ALL ABOUT ACTIVITIES

Cultural activities
Introduction of our home countries (culture, music, people...)
Parties:
Basket parties, which you make your own home made food (but make it double!) and bring it to ICOT  basement and let the other members to try it then in just one party you have tested several meals from different countries... WOW
You can book ICOT  basement for your own parties and activities
Music & dance parties
Games and Sports: We have some kind of sport (Football, Table tennis, Dart, chess, etc) competitions every year on Christmas period
Scientific: Visit museums , scientific trips, and short courses
Film Show: Every year we try to show number of movies from different countries
Trips: Every year we try to have a trip to neighboring countries or inside Norway. During Easter Holidays a international trip is usually planned
and more!

Annual General Meeting & Election For New Executive Committee
Typically in September or October
All members are requested to be present
Pizza party held afterwards
Renewal of membership and new membership can be taken on the same day
Movie on big screen afterwards

ICOT International Film Festival
