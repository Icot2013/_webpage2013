The International Club of Trondheim (ICOT) is a non-profit, non-political and purely a social organization of the students and their families, established by a group of international students studying at NTNU.
To organise cultural and social activities from time to time.
To conduct Seminars Short courses and speeches on different issues.
To indoor and outdoor sport activities among members, their family and children.
Co-ordinate and contact with NORAD, NTNU, Commune and other clubs in Norway.

Background                                                                Objectives
The ICOT was established in the year 1973 and has its registered office at Moholt Alle-16 (Basement), Moholt Student Town, NTNU, Trondheim, Norway.
Following is the list of countries that have been represented:
Bangladesh , Bhutan , China , Congo, Ethiopia, Ghana, India, Iran, Maldive, Nepal, Nigeria, Norway, Pakistan, Palestine, Slovakia, Spain, Sri Lanka, Tanzania, USA, Uganda, Vietnam, Zambia
ICOT is mainly supported by office of international relations of NTNU and it promotes and organizes various social and cultural activities with prime interest of cultural exchange and friendship among all members.
ICOT welcomes all its present membersvand the prospective members to extend the hands of cooperation with some great ideas and constructive minds to achieve its objectives for which it is established. ICOT will definitely try to provide its members a good activities and opportunities to cherish their entire stay in Trondheim.
