Anyone who is the member of the club can participate in the activities of the club. Membership is open to all who is willing to obey the rules and regulation of the club and after paying the annual membership fee.

The club is run by the contributions from its members, donations, grants and help from NORAD and local commune.

The administrative activities are governed by an executive body of the club that is selected every year through certain procedure of election among its members.

The club has own constitution and the general council or the executive body of the club acts within its constitutional framework.
